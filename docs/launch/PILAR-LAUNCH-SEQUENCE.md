# PILAR 1-4 Launch Sequence (2026-H2)

Four sellable pillars shipped between Sprint 30-33. Each gets its own
launch dalga — landing-page hero update, cold-email campaign, demo
video, case-study slot. Pillars deliver **independent ROI stories**
so they don't cannibalise each other.

## Pillar map → buyer pain → ROI hook

| # | Pillar | Buyer | Pain | Hook |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Ambient Clinical Scribe | Clinician | 8-15 hrs/week documentation | "Save 10 hours a week. Get your evenings back." |
| **2** | Measurement-Based Care Engine | Group practice owner | Insurance reimbursement leverage | "+5-15% payer reimbursement (CMS MIPS #134, CalAIM, Cigna VBC)." |
| **3** | No-Show Predictor + Auto-Recovery | Practice manager | 15-25% no-show bleed | "Save ~$2k/month per clinician. ROI calculator on the landing page." |
| **4** | Treatment Plan Drafter | Group practice + new clinicians | Onboarding speed + liability | "Cut plan-drafting time 50%. SMART goals cited to NICE/APA." |

## Launch order (sales-friendly cadence)

1. **Week 0 — PILAR 1 launch (the emotional hero)**
   - Landing hero swap to scribe demo video
   - Cold-email Wave A: "Press record. Get a signed SOAP note in 60s." → 2k clinician list
   - One free 30-day trial offer attached to the headline
2. **Week 2 — PILAR 3 launch (the ROI calculator)**
   - Landing pricing page adds the ROI banner: "Save ~$2k/month per clinician or your trial extends another 30 days"
   - Cold-email Wave B targeted at practice managers (not clinicians)
3. **Week 4 — PILAR 2 launch (the enterprise unlock)**
   - Press release: "First mental-health EHR to ship MBC-certified PHQ-9/GAD-7 dispatching"
   - Reach-out to 30 multi-clinician practices on Headway / Alma / Sondermind
4. **Week 6 — PILAR 4 launch (the moat statement)**
   - Long-form blog: "We don't just generate plans. We generate plans that cite NICE CG90."
   - LinkedIn carousel breakdown of the 12 protocols + co-sign workflow

## What we DON'T say

- Don't reference a single founder. Brand voice is **"we / our team / the platform"** — EU-based.
- Don't promise FDA clearance. Drafter + scribe are **CDS non-device** (clinician edits + signs).
- Don't claim payer contracts we don't have. CMS MIPS #134 is the **published reimbursement vehicle**, not a guarantee of any specific payer.
- Don't price-anchor against Stripe-billing competitors until our own billing ships.
