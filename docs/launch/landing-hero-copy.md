## Operational notes

- **A/B/C order**: start with the variant whose hook matches the target list's known pain. (Cold lists → emotional A. Practice-owner lists → quantified C.)
- **Image policy**: hero image is the product screen, not a stock therapist. We sell the tool, not the persona.
- **Below-the-fold**: "Trusted by EU-based clinicians and US group practices" — never a single quoted testimonial until we have one in writing.
- **Pricing block**: don't ship a price tag until billing is live. Today the CTA goes to a waitlist or trial-request form.
